TasteBud

TasteBud creates new customers and offsets static overhead for restaurants by offering variable discounts at off-peak hours. Over the last three years, I have worked with them to redesign and rebrand their app, and further explore new app features.

Specimens

Following the app's soft launch, we spent the Summer of 2014 rebranding Azul. I worked with the company's founder to iterate names and brand characters, and we eventually hired Greg Anthony Thomas to create and perfect the TasteBud logotype, while I worked on the app icon.
